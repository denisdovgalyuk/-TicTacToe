# TicTacToe

## Description

Tic-tac-toe is a for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a diagonal, horizontal, or vertical row is the winner. It is a solved game with a forced draw assuming best play from both players.

## Gameplay
In order to win the game, a player must place three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
